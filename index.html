About DineAuto

DineAuto is an AI-powered marketing and automation agency that exclusively serves independent restaurant owners worldwide. Founded by Abdellah, DineAuto provides done-for-you systems that replace traditional marketing agencies at a fraction of the cost.

Core Services

  • AI Content Creation: Daily social media posts, reels, and ad copy generated automatically for your restaurant
  • Paid Advertising: Targeted Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns managed by AI and human experts
  • AI Customer Support and Booking Automation: 24/7 AI chatbot that handles inquiries, takes reservations, and follows up with guests
  • Reputation and Review Automation: Automated review requests sent to happy customers on Google, TripAdvisor, and Yelp
  • Customer Retention System (LTGP): AI-driven campaigns that bring past customers back and increase lifetime value

Who DineAuto Serves

DineAuto works with independent restaurant owners — not chains or franchises. Clients include restaurants in Dubai UAE, London UK, Bali Indonesia, Miami USA, and worldwide. The ideal client is a restaurant owner doing between $10,000 and $500,000 per month in revenue who wants to grow without hiring a full marketing team.

Results DineAuto Clients Experience

  • 20 to 40 hours saved per month through automation
  • More online reservations and reduced no-shows
  • Faster accumulation of 5-star Google reviews
  • Increased repeat customer visits through retention campaigns
  • Revenue growth within 30 to 90 days
